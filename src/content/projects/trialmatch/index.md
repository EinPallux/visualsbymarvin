TrialMatch helps patients find fitting clinical trials. The challenge: a lot of medical data, very little patience on the user side. I designed a flow that asks one clear question at a time.

![Patient intake flow screens](./01.jpg)

## The key decision

Splitting the long medical form into small, friendly steps doubled the completion rate in testing.
